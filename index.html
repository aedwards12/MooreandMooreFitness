Round 10

Class entails one hour of cardio, boxing and core and ab training providing a total body workout in one hour. Come try our class and jab, cross, hook and uppercut on a heavy bag and pads to a healthier and stronger new you.

MMF Kickboxing

One hour class is a combination of cardio boxing and martial arts providing an overall total body workout aiming to improve strength, aerobic fitness, speed, flexibility, eye and hand coordination and balance. Class utilizes gloves, heavy bags and pads.

Muscle Burn

One hour of high repetition strength training ,plyometrics, and core training. Strength Training offers high repetition strength training with dumbbells for toning and conditioning.Plyometrics training activates, increase muscular power and explosiveness.Core Training improves balance and stability within the muscles in your pelvis, lower back, hips, back, and abs.

Round 10

This invirgorating workout is the intense version of Rounds with intervals of muscle and core conditioning in a station format. In this class you use gloves, heavy bags, pads , dumbbells, medicine and stability balls, and resistance bands.

HIIPE-X60

High Intensity Interval, Plyometric, Endurance workout. It combines a challenging, fun, yet effective cardiovascular routines interlaced with running, drills, stairs, plyometrics, calisthenics.

About Us

MooreandMoore Fitness Studio provides personalized and group physical fitness training for people ranging from their teens to their golden years. We offer a variety of small group fitness classes ranging from various dance fitness classes, cardio classes, kickboxing classes, boxing/cardio, and light weight training/cardio classes. In addition, individual fitness training will be offered in 30-60 minute sessions, as well as boxing training in 30-60 minute sessions.

Donna Moore is a certified personal trainer with many years experience in the areas of leading of wide variety of group fitness classes. She also has experience in the training and oversight of new instructors. In addition, Donna Moore has had several years experience in personal training. She has a great ability to recognize the optimum workout plan for each of her specific clients, and does a great job in personally bonding with her clients as well.

Michael Moore is also extremely dedicated to physical fitness. He is a certified personal trainer and a certified boxing trainer. He is also very good at motivating people to achieve their fitness goals, and is always increasing his knowledge of personal training.

Both Michael and Donna have trained with former boxing world champions Livingstone Bramble and Billy Costello, and share these experiences while training clients.
